Why Trees Are Important

Trees are very important for our environment. They provide oxygen and help reduce pollution. Planting trees makes the earth cleaner and healthier.

Everyone should plant trees in schools, homes, and public places. More trees mean better rainfall, fresh air, and a beautiful future for all living beings.

Plant today for a greener tomorrow!
