Introduction to nature

Nature is the most valuable gift given to humans. It includes forests, rivers, mountains, animals, and the atmosphere around us.Nature provides peace, beauty, and life support to all living beings.Protecting nature is very important for future generations.

Trees produce oxygen and help reduce pollution. Rivers provide fresh water, while forests support wildlife. Human beings depend on natural resources every day for food, shelter, and energy.
